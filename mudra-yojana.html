Mudra Yojana

About the Scheme

The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) offers microfinance loans to women entrepreneurs who wish to start a small business or expand their existing business.

Benefits

  • Three loan categories:
  • Loan (Up to ₹50,000) – For small businesses.
  • Kishor Loan (₹50,000 – ₹5 lakh) – For growing businesses.
  • Loan (₹5 lakh – ₹10 lakh) – For larger expansions.
  • No collateral required for loans up to ₹10 lakh.
  • Encourages women-led startups in small-scale industries.

Eligibility

  • Women running small businesses, self-help groups, and startups.
  • Business should be in manufacturing, services, or agriculture-related sectors.
