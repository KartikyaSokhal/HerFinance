Mahila E-Haat

About the Scheme

Mahila E-Haat is a digital platform for women entrepreneurs to showcase and sell their products directly. Launched by the Ministry of Women and Child Development in 2016, it enables women entrepreneurs, self-help groups (SHGs), and NGOs to market and sell their products online.

Benefits

  • No registration fee – Women entrepreneurs can display their products without any charges.
  • Direct market access – Helps women sell handmade products, handicrafts, organic items, clothing, etc., without middlemen.
  • Empowerment through technology – Encourages digital literacy and financial independence for women.
  • Provides an online marketing platform for women entrepreneurs.
  • Empowers women financially by enabling direct sales.

Eligibility

  • Open to women entrepreneurs, SHGs, and NGOs.
  • Must have products or services to sell.

How to Apply

Visit the official Mahila E-Haat website and register as a seller by providing necessary business details.
