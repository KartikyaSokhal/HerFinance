Financial Glossary

Asset

An asset is anything of value that you own, such as cash, property, or investments.

Liability

A liability is a financial obligation or debt that you owe to others.

Equity

Equity represents ownership in a company or the value of an asset after deducting liabilities.

Interest Rate

The interest rate is the cost of borrowing money, expressed as a percentage of the loan amount.

Diversification

Diversification is a risk management strategy that involves spreading investments across different assets.
