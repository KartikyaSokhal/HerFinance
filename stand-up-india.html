Stand Up India

About the Scheme

Launched by the Government of India in 2016, this scheme provides bank loans to women entrepreneurs from SC/ST backgrounds or those setting up a new business in the manufacturing, services, or trading sector.

Benefits

  • Loan amount: ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore for starting a business.
  • Lower interest rates compared to regular loans.
  • Encourages women-led businesses in underrepresented sectors.
  • Repayment period: Up to 7 years with a moratorium of 18 months.

Eligibility

  • Women above 18 years.
  • Business must be new and not already established.
  • The applicant must own at least 51% of the business.

How to Apply

Apply online or visit a bank to seek guidance.
