Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana(SSY)

About the Scheme

A small savings scheme under the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao initiative, SSY helps parents save for their daughter’s education and marriage.

Benefits

  • High interest rates (currently around 8% per annum).
  • Tax-free savings under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
  • Partial withdrawals allowed after the girl turns 18 for education.
  • Minimum deposit ₹250, Maximum ₹1.5 lakh per year.

Eligibility

  • Open to girl children below 10 years.
  • Can be opened by parents/guardians.

How to Apply

Visit any bank or post office to open an SSY account.

Fill out the form and submit it with KYC documents.
